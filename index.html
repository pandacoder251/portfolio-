About Me

I am a passionate web developer with a keen interest in designing dynamic and responsive web applications. My journey in technology began with a simple curiosity about how websites function, which gradually evolved into a dedicated career. I thrive on transforming innovative ideas into practical solutions and continuously seek to expand my expertise by learning new technologies.
Beyond my technical pursuits, I have a strong creative side. I am enthusiastic about writing and doodling, and I secured the second position in a national-level fine arts competition, reflecting my commitment to artistic excellence. Currently, I am reading "Ikigai" and working on my novel, balancing my creative projects with academic assignments and ongoing studies.
My involvement in hackathons has been particularly rewarding—I participated in two events, reaching the top 100 teams in the internal round of SIH Nominations out of 500+ teams and gaining valuable experience in Hack Odisha. While we didn’t win, the learning outcomes were substantial.
In my leisure time, I enjoy playing chess with my sibling and exploring photography as a hobby. On campus, I am member of the English Cafe Club & SOA Literary Club which provide me with opportunities to collaborate and grow within the student community.
